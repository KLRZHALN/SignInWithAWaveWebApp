Sign in with a Wave

Patients with adermatolyphia can more readily authenticate themselves by using the Sign in with a Wave website. The loss of a fingerprint is known as aderma- tolyphia, and our goal is to develop an authentication method that will provide these individuals with an accurate way to authenticate securely. This project is being worked on by four driven girls who hope to encourage people to take attention to dermatoglyphics, especially in this modern age. We are pleased to introduce the OTP generator, which enables users to quickly and easily authent- ticate via hand signals. By utilizing hand gestures that match the 4-digit code and delivering an OTP, you can rapidly authenticate yourself. Additional conc- erns include individuals who can access this website but have disabilities such as cognitive impairments or visual impairments. You can also encourage others to utilize this project as a model to add this type of authentication protocol elsewhere, similar to face ID and fingerprint authentication.
